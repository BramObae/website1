Platform Capabilities

Everything accountability needs.

Patients, doctors, activists, and the Ministry — one platform for transparent, evidence-based healthcare accountability in all 47 counties.

🛡️
Anonymous & Safe
Stay completely anonymous or identify yourself. Protected under Kenya's Whistleblower Act. Zero IP logging on anonymous reports.
📹
Upload Evidence
Attach photos, videos, receipts, discharge letters — real proof that makes complaints impossible to ignore or dismiss.
🏥
Hospital Spotlight
Facilities with repeated violations are publicly listed. Full transparency. Ratings, complaint counts, response history.
🏛️
Ministry Response Portal
Ministry posts official resolutions, disciplinary actions, and policy updates. No more "we didn't receive the notice."
📊
County-Level Tracking
Health is devolved. All 47 counties tracked separately. County governors receive county-specific reports.
💸
Patient Support Fund
Donate via M-Pesa to patients stuck over hospital fees. 100% goes to the patient. Real names, real stories.
⚡
Emergency Escalation
Life-threatening neglect gets a red-flag routing to the Ministry emergency desk and media contacts within minutes.
✊
Lobby & Activism Board
Doctors post strike notices. Activists mobilise demos. Lobby groups serve formal petitions — all timestamped and public.
📱
SMS / USSD Access
No smartphone? Report via SMS to 21900 or USSD *384#. Every Kenyan can participate, not just those with data.
